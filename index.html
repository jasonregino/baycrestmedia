Questions

Good questions, answered.

How much of my time does this take?

About five minutes a week. You send a voice memo, a few photos, or a quick note, a new listing, an open house, a market update, whatever's going on. We handle the writing, the formatting, and the platform versions.

Will it actually sound like me?

Yes. During onboarding we capture your tone, your phrases, and the way you talk about your work. Everything we write is built to sound like you, never generic or robotic.

Do you run ads or guarantee leads?

No. We are a content service, not an ads agency. Our job is to keep you visible and credible online with steady, professional content. We do not run paid ads or promise leads.

Is the content compliant, and do I review it before it posts?

Everything is compliance-aware and written to best practices: no invented facts, and for regulated fields like real estate, Fair-Housing-safe language with the price and street address held back. You review and approve everything before it posts, and on Done For You we never publish without your sign-off. For real estate, we recommend a quick check with your broker first. We follow best practices, but this is a drafting aid, not a legal guarantee.

What if I do not like a post?

Just tell us and we adjust until it feels right. Since every plan is month to month, you are never locked in, and we would rather get it right than have you post something that does not feel like you.

Which platforms do you cover?

Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok, X, and email, all from one quick update. We focus on the platforms that matter for your business so you are not spread thin.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Every plan is month to month with no long contracts. You stay because it is working, not because you are locked in.

What do you need from me to get started?

Just the raw material. A voice memo, a few photos, or a topic you want to talk about. We take it from there and send back a full week of content ready to publish.

Can I get just a website, or just review management, without a content plan?

Yes. Every service stands on its own, so you can start with just one and add more later only if you want. If you do want both a website and review management, the Local Presence Package bundles them for a better rate. Nothing is ever forced.

Do you build websites from scratch or use templates?

Custom, built for your business. We start from a premium base we have refined, then shape the design, content, and structure around you. It is fast and mobile-friendly, and it ships with the technical details that help you get found on Google and the AI assistants.

How does the Google review system actually work?

After you close with a client, we invite them to leave a review through the systems you already use, so the request reaches them at home. We ask every client, monitor what comes in, reply each week, and flag anything negative with a calm draft reply. You get more reviews without chasing anyone.
