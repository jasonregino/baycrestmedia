Privacy Policy

Effective July 23, 2026

Bay Crest Media ("Bay Crest Media," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to baycrestmedia.com and the services we provide.

Information We Collect

We collect information you give us directly when you contact us or request a service, such as your name, email address, phone number, and any details you share about your business. We also collect basic technical information automatically, such as your device type, browser, and pages visited, through analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide the services you request
  • To contact you by phone, email, or text about your inquiry or project
  • To send appointment confirmations, reminders, and follow-ups
  • To improve our website and services

SMS and Text Messaging

When you provide your phone number and opt in through a form on this website, you consent to receive text messages from Bay Crest Media related to your inquiry. This may include replies to missed calls, answers to your questions, appointment confirmations and reminders, and follow-up about the services you requested.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to opt out, or reply HELP for assistance.

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number or your SMS consent with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing purposes. Mobile information gathered for text messaging will not be shared with any third parties for marketing or promotional reasons.

How We Share Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate our business, such as hosting, analytics, or messaging platforms, and only to the extent needed to provide those services. We may also disclose information if required by law.

Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and analytics tools, such as Google Analytics, to understand how visitors use our site. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You can opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP. You can ask us to update or delete your information by emailing us at the address below.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when we post the updated version on this page.
